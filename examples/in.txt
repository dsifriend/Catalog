Format Description
    Items-1 New items are delimited by blank lines (or start of the file).
    Items-2 All whitespace is preserved in an item's title.
    Attributes-1 Each attribute goes directly after a new item or attribute.
    Attributes-2 Attributes delimit their name and contents w/ whitespace once.
    Attributes-3 The contents of an attribute, however long, must lie completely within a single line.

An Example
       att An attribute.
   feature A feature (another attribute).
